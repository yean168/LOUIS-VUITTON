About LV Brand

Founded in 1957, LV Brand is a luxury fashion brand that has consistently embraced the Maison de Luxury movement. Established as a result of the collaboration between the two brands, the Maison and Louis Vuitton, LV Brand has been recognized as one of the most prestigious and innovative fashion brands in the world.
