Crossbody Bags

Louis Vuitton’s cross-body bags are imbued with the Maison’s emblematic style codes, offering effortless adaptability. The distinctive designs take on a myriad of silhouettes, adorned in various materials and eye-catching details to create modern versatility.

Mini Bags

In miniaturized variations, the Maison's quintessential codes are embodied through small yet audacious creations. Adorned with Louis Vuitton's signature motifs and materials, each model is a contemporary personification of heritage savoir-faire.

Bumbags and Slingbags

Louis Vuitton’s collection of bumbags and slingbags unveils effortless functionality for on-the-go lifestyles. In a display of the Maison’s iconic style codes, the adaptable, hands-free designs, adorned in signature motifs and materials, offer a contemporary aesthetic for dynamic, everyday looks.

Backpacks
